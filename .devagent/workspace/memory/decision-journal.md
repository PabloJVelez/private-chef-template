# Decision Journal

Central log for mission-level and strategic decisions. Each entry captures context, who owns follow-up, and impacted artifacts.

## When to Use
Reserve for **significant strategic decisions**: mission/vision changes, architecture or process changes, policy that affects multiple teams or long-term direction. Do not use for routine path fixes, date refreshes, or minor copy edits.

## Entry Template
- `## YYYY-MM-DD — <Title>`
- Facilitator(s), Decision summary, Artifacts updated, Follow-up owners, Related clauses, Notes
